# Plan on it

## Elevator

Plan on it app will allow users to help plan their parties with a diy ease.  
  

## Audience


The target audience is for people with little to no experience on how to plan a party.  This could be planning a simple birthday party, to a grand opening.  I want the site to be user friendly and easy to follow directions. 

## Benefits

* Start planning a party in minutes. Have all the resources at your finger tip.
* Have a list of all your on planning events that happen various times of the year
* Even event planners can use this site to keep track of their own events to make sure they are on track.
* Collaborate with fellow co-worker/friends/clients to divide the work up to make sure it gets done.

## Features

* Users can sign in/sign out
* Users can search a party theme and start customizing their party.  You can choose from multiple categories, such as decorations, food, dessert, and ect.  Within these categories, you can choose the level of expectations that you want for your party.
* You can collaborate different projects with different people and track the progress.  You can also upload receipts to keep track of expenses.

 
## Technology

* Use react to create a single page application to load the users main screen to keep them on track.
* use eventbrite api to track event attendees through the app.
* make a lot of data
* have a picture gallery to inspire users
